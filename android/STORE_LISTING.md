DoVe risolve questo problema. Inserisci il sestiere e il numero civico, e l'app ti mostra esattamente dove si trova sulla mappa 3D di Venezia.

- Tema chiaro, scuro o automatico
- Disponibile in italiano e inglese

- Nessuna registrazione richiesta

## ENGLISH

### Title (max 30 char)
DoVe — Venice Civic Numbers

### Short description (max 80 char)
Find every Venice civic number: search, locate and navigate on the map.

### Full description (max 4000 char)
Venice has a unique civic numbering system: numbers follow the sestieri (districts), not the streets. An address like "Cannaregio 2345" doesn't tell you which calle, campo or fondamenta to look for — locals know by experience, everyone else gets lost.

DoVe solves this. Enter the sestiere and civic number, and the app shows you exactly where it is on a 3D map of Venice.

FEATURES:
- Search by civic number across all 7 sestieri: Cannaregio, Castello, Dorsoduro, Giudecca, San Marco, San Polo, Santa Croce
- Explore the lagoon islands: Murano, Burano, Torcello, Lido, Pellestrina, Sant'Erasmo, Vignole, Certosa, Sant'Elena, Sacca Fisola, Mazzorbo
- Interactive 3D map with building extrusions
- Direct navigation to the searched civic number
- Share location with anyone
- Distance calculation from your current position
- Browse streets and view all civic numbers by street
- Light, dark or system theme
- Available in Italian and English
- Works offline (civic data is bundled with the app)
- No registration required
- Zero ads
- No personal data collected

DoVe uses open data from the City of Venice and OpenStreetMap maps via OpenFreeMap. Developed with love for Venice.
