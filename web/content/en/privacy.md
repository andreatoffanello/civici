## 1. Data Controller

DoVe Venezia
Email: info@dovevenezia.com

## 2. Data Collected

**DoVe does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.**

The app works entirely offline: all 47,261 Venice civic numbers are bundled within the application package, and no communication with external servers takes place.

## 3. Location Data

The app may request access to the device's GPS location **solely** to display your position on the local map. Your location:

- Is never transmitted to DoVe or any third party
- Is never stored or logged
- Is used only locally, within the app
- You can use DoVe normally without granting this permission

## 4. Third-Party Services

DoVe uses **Apple MapKit** for map display. MapKit is managed by Apple Inc. and subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We do not use any other third-party SDKs.

## 5. Analytics and Tracking

DoVe does **not use** any analytics system, tracking, or usage statistics collection.

## 6. Local Storage

The app may save user preferences (such as language) and recent search history locally on your device. This data:

- Remains exclusively on your device
- Is never transmitted
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

## 7. Children

DoVe does not collect data and requires no registration. The app can be used by anyone, including children.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Since we do not collect email addresses, we will not send direct notifications.
